Adoption Success story

"I remember when I was little my dad was feeding alot of stray cats, since then every morning so many feral cats are waiting for us, some of them was in a bad condition and my dad use to take good care of them. I think being a pet lover was one of the traits I inherited from my father. Fast forward from 2011 when I saw Puma (my cat) in the street in a bad condition, no hesitation I took her to our house and gave her medicines, thankfully she survive. Puma has a unique set of eyes, one is blue and the other is yellowish brown. 2016 she gave birth to 3 cute little kittens and one them acquired her eyes which automatically become family's favorite. I named her Oreo and up until now she's with me. Oreo is a 6 years old cat now and currently pregnant. They say cat with those eye are expensive, but I will never sell her, no amount can match the joy and happiness she gave to my family. I always Cherrish her everyday"
